#### Section 6 — FAQ
Title: "Common questions"

Q: Is my email data safe?
A: Yes. NovaSift runs entirely on your machine. Your emails are 
fetched via IMAP directly to a local database. The only data that 
leaves your machine is what you send to your chosen AI provider 
(OpenAI, Gemini, etc.) using your own API key — we never see it.

Q: What AI providers are supported?
A: OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), Google Gemini, Groq, and NVIDIA NIM. 
You bring your own API key, so you're always in control of costs.

Q: How does the Pro license work?
A: After payment, you receive a license key via email. Open NovaSift, 
go to Settings → License, paste your key, and Pro features unlock 
instantly. One license works on up to 2 devices.

Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Yes. We offer a 7-day refund on monthly plans if you're not 
satisfied. Lifetime purchases are non-refundable after 14 days. 
See our refund policy for details.

Q: Does it work on Windows and Mac?
A: Yes. NovaSift is built with Electron and supports Windows 10+ 
and macOS 11+. Linux support is planned for a future release.

## TASK 2: Privacy Policy Page

Create page at /privacy

Title: "Privacy Policy"
Last updated: June 2026
Company: The Side Job (thesidejob.tech)

Content:

### 1. Introduction
NovaSift is a product of The Side Job (thesidejob.tech). This 
Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you 
use NovaSift, our AI-powered desktop email client.

### 2. Data We Do NOT Collect
NovaSift is built on a local-first, privacy-first architecture:
- We do not collect, store, or process your emails
- We do not store your Gmail credentials on our servers
- We do not log your AI API keys
- We do not track your email content or usage patterns
- Your email data never passes through our servers

### 3. Data We DO Collect
We collect only what's necessary to operate the license system:
- Your email address (provided at purchase, for license delivery)
- Your payment information (processed by Razorpay — we never 
  see your card details)
- Your license key and activation status (stored in our database 
  to validate Pro features)
- Basic crash reports via Sentry (anonymous, no email content)

### 4. How Your Email Data Works
NovaSift connects directly to Gmail via IMAP using credentials 
stored only on your local machine. Emails are stored in a local 
SQLite database on your device. AI processing uses your own API 
key sent directly to your chosen provider (OpenAI, Google, etc.) 
— The Side Job is never an intermediary.

### 5. Third-Party Services
- Razorpay: Payment processing (see razorpay.com/privacy)
- Resend: License key email delivery
- Sentry: Anonymous crash reporting
- Your chosen AI provider: Email analysis (using your own key)

### 6. Data Retention
License records are retained for as long as your account is active. 
You may request deletion by emailing support@thesidejob.tech.

### 7. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal 
data. Contact us at support@thesidejob.tech for any requests.

### 8. Contact
The Side Job · support@thesidejob.tech · thesidejob.tech
